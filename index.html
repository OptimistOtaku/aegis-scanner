CI/CD hard gate

Let deployment pass through the temple doors.

Aegis is designed to fail pipelines when findings meet your risk threshold and to emit terminal, markdown, or JSON reports.

aegis.yml 
- name: Aegis Security Scan
  run: npx @optimistotaku/aegis-scan . --fail-on HIGH --format markdown --output aegis-report.md

- name: Upload Aegis Report
  uses: actions/upload-artifact@v3
  with:
    name: aegis-security-report
    path: aegis-report.md
